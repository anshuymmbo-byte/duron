WHAT MAKES
DURON DIFFERENT

01

Zero-Wax Base Stock

Gas-to-liquid processing eliminates waxy compounds that cloud and thicken in the cold — giving you instant lubrication from the first crank, every time.

02

Anti-Oxidation Additive Pack

A blend of hindered phenol and diphenylamine antioxidants neutralizes free radicals generated under high heat — extending oil life by up to 30% compared to conventional grades.

03

Friction Modifier Complex

Organo-molybdenum compounds create a sacrificial boundary layer on cam lobes, rings, and bearings — reducing wear metals in used-oil analysis by a measurable margin.

04

Detergent-Dispersant System

Metallic sulfonates and succinimide dispersants keep combustion by-products in suspension, preventing varnish deposits and sludge — even in stop-and-go urban riding conditions.

Manufacturing Process

FROM SOURCE TO
ENGINE

1

Natural Gas Sourcing

Ultra-pure natural gas feedstock is selected from certified sources — the foundation of our Group IV base oil quality.

2

Fischer-Tropsch Synthesis

Gas is catalytically converted into a wax-free hydrocarbon liquid with a near-perfect molecular structure and no natural impurities.

3

Titanium Infusion

Proprietary nano-titanium particles are dispersed into the base oil under precision-controlled temperature and shear conditions.

4

Additive Blending

Antioxidants, friction modifiers, detergents, and VI improvers are metered in batch reactors to precise formulation targets.

5

Quality Certification

Every batch passes 48-point laboratory testing before receiving API, JASO, and BS6 certification stamps — zero exceptions.

DURON TECHNOLOGY

WHY DURON

01

Cold Start Mastery

Special flow improver additives ensure instant lubrication even at low temperatures — protecting your engine from the very first revolution.

02

Thermal Stability

Engineered for high-temperature stability up to 150°C, Duron maintains viscosity and protection under the most demanding riding conditions.

03

Titanium Technology

Proven Titanium technology creates a micro-thin layer on metal surfaces, chemically reducing wear and extending engine life.

04

Sludge Prevention

Advanced cleaning additives actively prevent sludge formation, keeping your engine internals spotlessly clean for long-term reliability.

05

Fuel Efficiency

The friction-reducing formulation minimises energy loss in the engine, translating directly into real-world fuel savings on every ride.

06

PurePlus Technology

Synthesised from natural gas, PurePlus base oil delivers exceptional purity — free from impurities that compromise conventional lubricants.

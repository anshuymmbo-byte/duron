Our Story

MORE THAN OIL.
A LEGACY.

Founded in 1999, Duron Engine Oil began with a single promise — to give every Indian engine the protection it truly deserves. From our first production line in a small facility to a pan-India distribution network spanning Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities, our journey has been built on one thing: trust.

We craft our lubricants using advanced additive technology, precision-blended to meet international API and JASO standards. Whether it's a two-wheeler on mountain roads or a commercial fleet on national highways — Duron delivers.
